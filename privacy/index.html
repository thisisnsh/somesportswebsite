Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version: we store the minimum needed to sign you in, your picks and settings stay on your device, everything else is anonymous, we don't sell your data, and you can ask us to delete your profile any time.

What we collect

  • Your profile: when you sign in with Google we store your name, email address, and profile photo URL. We use Google Sign-In; we never see your password.
  • Anonymous analytics: basic usage and crash data (via Firebase Analytics/Crashlytics) so we can keep the app working during the tournament. This is anonymized — it's tied to a random app-instance identifier, never to your name or sign-in.
  • Celebration taps: when you tap the ball after a goal, your taps are counted toward a worldwide total for that match. These are anonymized and only ever stored as aggregate numbers — never tied to you.

What we don't collect

  • Any personal information beyond what is listed above.
  • The teams you follow and your app settings (theme, which events you want to see) stay on your device — we never send them to our server.

Where it lives

Your profile is stored on Google Cloud (Firestore) in the United States. Anonymous analytics are processed by Firebase.

Deleting your data

Email support@alldayidreamaboutsports.com and we'll delete your profile for you.
