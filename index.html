How do I get live World Cup 2026 scores on my Mac?

Download All Day I Dream About Sports — a free macOS app. It delivers live goal, card, penalty, substitution and full-time alerts right below your MacBook notch, and pins a live score capsule to any corner of your screen. No extra browser tab, no phone in your hand.

Is All Day I Dream About Sports free?

Yes — it's completely free on macOS 14 and later. A Windows version is coming soon; join the waitlist from the download button.

Which matches, leagues and competitions are covered?

It launches with the FIFA World Cup 2026, following every match you choose. The Premier League, La Liga, UEFA Champions League, Bundesliga and more sports are coming soon.

What kinds of live football notifications does it show?

Goals, yellow and red cards, penalties, VAR checks, substitutions, kickoff and full-time — every key moment, delivered as a quiet drop under your notch so live score alerts never break your focus.

Will live score notifications interrupt my work?

No. Alerts slide in below the notch and get out of the way, and a floating score capsule keeps the running score a glance away. It's built to keep you in flow while you follow the match.

Can I celebrate goals with other fans in real time?

Yes. The moment your team scores, the goal takes over your screen — tap the ball to celebrate live with football fans across the globe, all at once.

Is it affiliated with FIFA?

No. It is not affiliated with FIFA or any league, club or brand. Team names and crests are used only to identify the matches you choose to follow.
