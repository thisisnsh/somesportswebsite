Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version: we store the minimum needed to sync your team picks across Macs, we sell nothing, and you can delete everything with one button.

What we collect

  • Google account basics when you sign in: your name, email address, and profile photo URL. We use Google Sign-In; we never see your password.
  • Your preferences: the teams you follow and app settings (theme, notification choices), so they roam with you.
  • Celebration taps: when you tap the ball after a goal, we add your tap count to an anonymous worldwide counter for that match. The counter stores numbers, not identities.
  • Crash reports and anonymous usage analytics (Firebase Crashlytics/Analytics) to keep the app working during the tournament.

What we don't collect

  • No contacts, files, browsing history, or location.
  • No screen contents — goal animations are even hidden from your own screen recordings and screen shares.
  • No ads, no data sales, no third-party marketing. Ever.

Where it lives

Data is stored on Google Cloud (Firestore) in the United States. Push notifications are delivered through Apple's push service and Firebase Cloud Messaging.

Deleting your data

Settings → Delete account removes your profile and preferences immediately and permanently. Or email support@alldayidreamaboutsports.com and we'll do it for you.
